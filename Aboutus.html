Who are we?


We are TOUFIK BAGWAN & ASSOCIATES, & we are a registered firm since 2017
And since then we have contributed towards various projects across Maharashtra to provide
a better space for our clients.

We do that by creating groundbreaking projects,by building a diverse and passionate team, by
partnering with the best in the industry, and by making a positive impact in our communities where we live and work.

Today, we are recognized as leaders in the industry, for our strength in traditional construction
methods and for our creative,fresh approach to cutting edge technologies and delivery systems.
